Welcome to MyLoweLife of Programming!

I'm Tony Lowe and I've begun building this site from the ground up as a way to develop new skills as well as showcase what I've learned on my coding journey. I will be progressively improving this site as I experiment with new technologies and attempt to write new programs.

During the pandemic I watched countless people I know try and fail countless times to obtain various items but especially PS5s.

I came to realize, just like sneakers that get resold for excessive amounts of money, a collective of people were using bots to profit off of other people by buying up all the inventories. These bots are usually thousands of dollars which doesn't make them a viable option for many consumers when it would only be needed for that occassional hot item.

Now, many of you may not know what a "bot" is. Simply put, a bot is a program that performs tasks as a human would but at speeds no human can ever achieve in an automatic fashion.

My goal is to bring this sort of bot to more people for little to nothing per purchase but with limits so the system won't be abused.

I will be running a free beta program for a limited number of people as I develop the software.
